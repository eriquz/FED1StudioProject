About Us

We are a trusted company for over 30 years to teach Kansas City about the fundamentals of technology! Our course goes over computer hardware, operating systems, software, security, configuring workstations, and safety! After our course you will be able to branch out your education, our goal is to get you fundamentally prepared to be working with tech!
